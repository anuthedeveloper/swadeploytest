1. The Firefly squid

The firefly squid, Watasenia scintillans, is a mesmerizing marine species inhabiting the western Pacific Ocean. These small cephalopods, about 3 inches in length, emit bioluminescent light, creating a stunning display in Toyama Bay, Japan, during their annual mating season. Their captivating glow is a result of a chemical reaction in specialized light organs.
