1. Greek pottery

Greek pottery stands as a testament to ancient artistic prowess. Employing techniques like black-figure and red-figure painting, artisans depicted mythological narratives, daily life, and gods on vessels like amphorae and kylixes. Notable styles include the geometric, black-figure, and red-figure periods, showcasing evolving artistic skills. These pots served practical purposes, such as storing liquids or participating in religious ceremonies, while also contributing significantly to our understanding of Greek culture and aesthetics.
